What is legal translation?

Legal translation is a translation that has been formally verified for use in official purposes. Documents that require certified translation services might include papers that have to be submitted to Ministry of Manpower and Immigration or Ministry of Justice (such as birth, marriage and divorce certificates), official transcripts (such as high school certificates, university degrees and vocational training certificates).
