Rights for all

Law Firm

Rights for all is a new law firm located in Egypt which provides full legal services to its clients concerning the Egyptian,
Regional and International legal market.

Rights For All ( Mazen Abdul-Qader & Co.) is a new midsize Law Firm located in Cairo. While most of us are Egyptians, we are all international and global in our know-how, experience and practice. Our Law Firm consists of two partners and more than 18 lawyers, translators and trainers. Our specializations are primarily in Corporate, Investment, Commercial, Sports and Civil matters. In addition, Our Law Firm deals with a number of related criminal issues. Due to the variety of our team experience and knowledge in the Legal Market, we are also specialized in many practice areas of law such as; Family, Intellectual Property, Mediation and Arbitration. Moreover, we believe that providing professional legal training could bring the best lawyers and legal translators in the Legal Market, therefore we have contributed to qualify and create new generation of lawyers and legal translators by our legal courses and our effective contribution in the legal community. Our team is highly-professional, highly-qualified & well-experienced lawyers, lawyer-linguists , legal translators and holding post-graduate, PhDs, Masters degrees in Law and Legal translation. Finally, Legal translation became one of the crucial tools needed in the Regional and International Legal Market, we have a department which provides a high quality of legal translation. Our legal translation Department is divided into both lawyer-linguists and legal translators.
