Legal Consultation

Our services include the following:


Contract

Our firm offers individualized contract services from negotiations to contract drafting to dispute resolution in civil and commercial law.

IP, Media & Press

Rights for all advises its clients on any copyright related issues arising from their activities. The firm makes sure that issues related to moral rights will not prevent our customers from marketing their copyrighted works or maximizing their value. The firm offers a full range of legal, procedural, consulting and managerial trademark protection services. Our services are meant to help our clients meet their specific trademark protection needs. Rights for all has also specialized lawyers in press law and broadcast agreements. The firm advises businesses and private individuals, and especially photographers, journalists, programmers and web designers, about their rights and obligations.


Family

Rights for all advises and guides our clients who are facing problems including divorce and child custody. The firm focuses on conciliation as an alternative way to more intensive formal court hearing. During conciliation, the parties agree to meet with a conciliator, who is a neutral third party person trained to handle dispute resolution. The parties meet separately with the conciliator in attempts to resolve the dispute. The conciliator helps to do this by facilitating communication, interpreting factual and legal issues, providing advice, and suggesting potential solutions. The conciliator may also propose a negotiated settlement to each side for their approval.

Property

Rights for all provides a range of services, including eminent domain, commercial and residential real estate, land use, property tax appeals, commercial litigation, mortgage law, business transactions/litigation, eminent domain and estate planning.


Corporate & Investment

Rights for all establishes corporate entities like joint stock companies, limited liability companies, branches, representative offices, etc. The firm offers drafting service for BOD and OGM minutes, extracts commercial registers and tax cards and obtains the license(s) required for the legal entity to practice its activity in Egypt. Rights for all conducts due diligence reports for mergers & acquisitions and represents our clients before the competent authorities in order to finalize the transaction.

Arbitration

Rights for all advises its clients on the enforcement of arbitration awards and carries out all the necessary procedures to enforce them. The firm also drafts negotiations and reviews all arbitration clauses.


Sports

Rights for all has extensive knowledge of FIFA statutes and Egyptian sports laws. Our practice areas in sports law are sports disputes & ADR, sports investment and the criminal aspects of sports law.

Construction

Rights for all is mainly specialized in FIDIC and BOT agreements.


Immigration

Rights for all advises on immigration Law and apply for tourist and multiple entry visa and for work permits.

Employment

Rights for all drafts and reviews employment agreements (including non-compete clauses). The firm drafts settlement agreements. The firm advises on labor issues including employment termination, investigation with an employee, annual raise, employees' redundancy. The firm reviews employment policies and internal manuals. Rights for all can also open insurance file for the company and its employees. We primarily resort to negotiation and mediation as we believe that these methods are the most cost effective.
